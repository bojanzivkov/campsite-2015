About Iron Camp

Iron Camp is a 4-days long huge Drupalcamp about Central- and Eastern Europe’s Drupal scene for everyone who is interested in what this area can show off in the related field. The event is going to be a conference and workshop to get together Drupal professionals and the community to the Central-Eastern European region. We would like to bring together the region’s Drupal community to share the knowledge and love of Drupal and to learn from Drupal experts from all over the world. Our unconcealed goal is also to bring business to this area that helps developing our foundations and provides a continuous flow in experience and quality work.

Iron Camp is both for local or farther professionals. If you would like to dive into the community life or would like to find the best partner for your business, this is your place to be. We are offering various occasions for both!

Iron Camp is brought to you by the area’s local communities.

Save the date! 26-30th October 2015, Budapest!
